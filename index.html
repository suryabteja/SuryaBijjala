Current Research

Surya T. Bijjala is a graduate student pursuing his PhD in Materials Science under the guidance of Dr. Pankaj Kumar at the University of New Mexico (2021–present). His research leverages density functional theory-guided Machine Learning and Powder Metallurgy-based manufacturing, alongside Gas Tungsten Arc Melting-based welding. His core expertise includes high-performance computations on the electronic structure of refractory alloys and attention-based ML models.
